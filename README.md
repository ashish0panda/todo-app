# My Journey - Todo List App

A modern, clean, and functional Todo List application built with Jetpack Compose and Material 3. Track your daily tasks and stay productive with a beautiful interface.

## ‚ú® Features

- **Task Management**: Easily add, edit, and delete tasks to keep your list up to date.
- **Progress Tracking**: Visual progress bar showing how many tasks you've completed in your "journey."
- **Home Screen Widget**: Stay updated without opening the app. A colorful Glance-based widget shows your active tasks directly on your home screen.
- **Task Reordering**: Organize your priorities by moving tasks up or down.
- **Persistent Storage**: All your data is saved locally using Room Database, so you never lose your progress.
- **Modern UI/UX**: Built with Jetpack Compose, featuring Material 3 design, smooth transitions, and haptic feedback.
- **Dark Mode Support**: Seamlessly adapts to your system theme.
